Her Impact

Ruth Bader Ginsburg was a United States Supreme Court Justice from 1993 until the year 2020. Ginsburg's fought hard for women's rights until the end.

She passionately dedicated herself to closing the gender gap. Her ambition and impact both are truly inspiring.

In fact, she argued a total of six cases regarding gender discrimination in front of the Supreme Court, for both men and women, and she won five.

"Women belong in all places where decisions are being made. It shouldn't be that women are the exception." -Ruth Bader Ginsburg
